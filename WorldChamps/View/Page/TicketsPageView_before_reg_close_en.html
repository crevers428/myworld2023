Tickets

Companion tickets

Competitors can buy companion tickets for guests attending the competition with them. Each competitor is allowed two 4-day companion tickets for 10 AUD each. Spectators and additional guests will be able to purchase tickets at the door at the prices below.

Spectator tickets

Spectators and additional guests can buy tickets at the event box office on competition days at the following prices:

  • 4-day adult ticket: 20 AUD
  • 4-day child (under 16) ticket: 10 AUD
  • 1-day adult ticket: 10 AUD
  • 1-day child (under 16) ticket: 5 AUD
  • All children under 6: free

Only cash payments will be accepted. All children under 16 years old must be supervised by an adult.
