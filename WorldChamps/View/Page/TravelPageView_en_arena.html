Travel

Venue

The competition will be held at Pabellón Multiusos 1 of Madrid Arena.

Address: Avda. de Portugal s/n
Nearest metro stop: Lago Línea 10
Set-up:

  • Main entrance: second floor
  • Bleachers: first floor
  • Competition and chilling area: Arena ground floor
  • Concentration rooms (FM, 4BLD, 5BLD, MBLD): rooms in ground floor
  • Fun Zone: second floor
  • Bar / Restaurant: first floor
  • Toilet: ground floor

Transportation

Nearest airport: Aeropuerto Madrid Barajas Adolfo Suárez, 21 km from Madrid Arena.

Public transportation: Metro is the cheapest, fastest and most comfortable transportation method in Madrid. The connection the airport to Madrid Arena is done through metro Línea 8 with a transfer at Nuevos Ministerios to metro Línea 10 until Lago in 45 minutes.

Other public transportation options:

  • Commuter train (RENFE)
  • Urban bus (EMT)
  • Taxi / Uber / Cabify
    Cabify is the Spanish alternative to Uber. Try it!

Some tips for getting around Madrid

  • Abono turístico de transporte (Touristy transportation bonus). If you or your family are going to visit the city, the "Abono turístico de transporte" Zona A includes the whole city and the airport, and it’s the best option for you.
  • If you are not going to travel a lot from the competition venue, the booklet of 10 metro tickets at €12,20 is the best option for you. It can be shared with others and rechargeable in a "Tarjeta multi" available at all the metro stations.
  • To visit the city center from the competition area, the best option is to travel using metro Línea 10 from Lago to Plaza de España.
  • Metro working hours: 6:00 - 1:30.
  • There’s also a night bus service departing from Plaza de Cibeles.
