Sure you know this feeling: you're on your way back home after several days of intense competition, and you think that, with all the stress, you didn't have time to chat calmly with other cubers.

This will not happen to you in WCA World Championship 2019 because we have the Fun Day!

July 23

Fun Day

The next Monday to the competition we will be in Parque de Atracciones Madrid, (Madrid Theme Park) located inside the Casa de Campo, only 15 minutes away walking from the competition venue.

In the park there are many attractions to enjoy together, many shows to watch, and lots of places where to chat calmly.

In case you still don't know, the summer in Spain is hooooot and sunny. Come prepared: bring cap and sunscreen, and prepare to hydrate continuously

Plans

Quite a simple plan, actually: we will meet at the entrance before the opening to enter like a group at a reduced price (€18,50 for adults +info about prices) and after that there are no more plans than hanging out with your friends. The park opens from noon to midnight.

You will have more details later in your email and in myWorlds.

How to arrive

Arriving can't be easier: the Batán metro stop of Línea 10 leaves you at the park entrance.

You can't miss the funniest day of WCA World Championship 2019!
