Koalafication Competitions



Speedcubing Australia and the Kubing Koala are running a series of Koalafication competitions around the country in order to allow local competitors to Koalify! for the Rubik's presents WCA World Championship 2019.

For more information about these competitions and to register to compete, please visit the competition pages below.

Registration spots are limited, so be sure to get in quick!
