Madrid, the city


Madrid is one of the most interesting, touristic and well communicated capitals in Europe. The Adolfo Suárez Madrid-Barajas Airport has direct connections with a large number of cities around Europe and around the globe, and virtually any existing airline flies to the city. The airport has direct communication with the city center through a wide range of cheap transportation methods, including direct access to Madrid Metro (underground), trains and buses.
