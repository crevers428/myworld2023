Hostels

Youth Hostel Richard Schirrmann

#nature #lowcost #connection

Address: Casa de Campo, s/n - 28011 Madrid

Info: It's the cheapest option, far from the noise of the city and surrounded by nature. It's also the closest hostel to the Glass Pavilion in the city, something that will allow you to skip using the public transport.

Web: www.madrid.org

Booking: reservas@madrid.org

Fees:

  • < 25 year old: 14€/person and night, bed and breakfast
  • 25-30 year old: 15.40€/person and night, bed and breakfast
  • > 30 year old: 19.80€/person and night, bed and breakfast

Connection to WCA World Championship 2019:

  • Best option: walking (~25 minutes)

Hostel Meeting Point

#tourism #leisure #pricequality

Address: Calle Factor, 5 - 28013 Madrid

Info: Located in front of Palacio Real and Catedral de la Almudena. If you want to get the most of your free time to visit the city center and its main historic monuments, this is your best located and cheaper option. Less than 25 minutes by public transport from the WCA World Championship 2019.

Web and Booking: www.hostelsmeetingpointmadrid.es/

Connection to WCA World Championship 2019:

  • 1st option (~10 minutes, 7 stops + 14 minutes walk): bus EMT 31
  • 2nd option (~13 minutes, 2 stops + 12 minutes walk): Metro Ramal from Ópera to Príncipe Pío and then Línea 10 to Lago

Mola Hostel

#tourism #party #pricequality

Address: Calle de Atocha, 16 - 28012 Madrid

Info: Alike the Meeting Point hostel, The Mola hostel is another economic option to accommodate groups in the city center. Only a few minutes from from Plaza Mayor and Puerta del Sol, and also near other main touristy attractions in the city. In the neighborhood of the Letters, the party nights in the mythical Huertas street are more than assured!

Web: www.molahostel.com

Booking: groups@molahostel.com

Fees:

Special offer for the WCA World Championship 2019 only. Make sure to mention the offer in your booking email! Fees for single beds in 8-person rooms. Fee per person and day is:

  • July 18: €25
  • July 19: €25
  • July 20: €23
  • July 21: €23

Only bed. Changes or cancellations are not allowed. Add breakfast for €5 per person and day.

Connection to WCA World Championship 2019:

  • 1st option (~22 minutes + 10 minutes walk): Metro Línea 1 from Tirso de Molina to Tribunal, and then Línea 10 to Lago
  • 2nd option (~20 minutes + 12 minutes walk): bus EMT 31

Hostel Era Alonso Martínez

#party #pricequality #connection

Address: Calle de Serrano Anguita, 11 - 28004 Madrid

Info: Located next to the historic district of Malasaña, famous for its many typical bars, restaurants, shops, and markets. It's the best option to combine culture, party, and speedcubing with friends. Only 20 minutes by public transport from the WCA World Championship 2019!

Web and Bookings: www.hostelera.es

Connection to WCA World Championship 2019:

  • Best option: (~10 minutes, 4 stops + 12 minutes walk): Metro Línea 10 from Alonso Martínez to Lago
